Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job?

To post a job, log in to your account, navigate to the "Post a Job" section, and fill in details like title, budget, deadline, and job description. Once submitted, freelancers can bid on your job.

How do I choose a freelancer?

You can review freelancer profiles, ratings, portfolios, and their bids. Choose the one that best matches your requirements and budget.

How do payments work?

Payments are made through a secure escrow system. Funds are held until the job is completed to your satisfaction, ensuring both parties are protected.

How do I bid on a job?

Go to the job listings page, select a job you're interested in, and submit your proposal with your budget and timeline. You can also add a custom message to showcase your expertise.

How is the rating system managed?

After job completion, clients can rate freelancers based on their performance. High ratings improve your visibility and trustworthiness on the platform.

When do I get paid?

Once the job is marked as complete by the client, the funds held in escrow are released to your account. Ensure your payment details are updated to withdraw funds.

How do admins manage disputes?

Admins can intervene in disputes by reviewing the project details, communication history, and evidence from both parties to provide a fair resolution.

How are fraudulent activities prevented?

We use a combination of automated checks and manual review processes to ensure profiles, projects, and transactions are authentic. Users can report suspicious activities directly to admins.

Can admins edit or remove job listings?

Yes, admins have the authority to edit or remove job listings that violate platform policies or contain inappropriate content.

What is the bidding process?

Freelancers submit proposals to clients specifying their budget and timeline. Clients can review these bids and select the most suitable freelancer for the job.

How are project milestones managed?

Clients can break projects into milestones. Payments are released in parts as milestones are completed and approved by the client.

Is there customer support?

Yes, our platform offers 24/7 customer support to assist with technical issues, disputes, and account management.
